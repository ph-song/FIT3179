Gross National Income (GNI) is used to determined a coutry's economic activities. It includes all income generated by the country's residents. According to The World Bank, country income is classified in to four categories based on GNI per capita and World Band Atlas method . The categories are low-income (below $1,085), lower-middle-income (between $1,086 to $4,255), upper-middle-income ($4,256 to $13,205) and upper-income (above $13,205).
